#  Travel Genie

> **Your AI-powered one-stop travel planning assistant.**

Travel Genie is an intelligent travel planning platform designed to eliminate the hassle of using multiple apps and websites for planning a trip. Whether you're traveling solo, with friends, family, or as a couple, Travel Genie creates a personalized travel experience based on your preferences.

##  Our Mission

Planning a trip shouldn't feel like a project.

Today, travelers switch between Google, Maps, weather apps, booking platforms, blogs, YouTube, and social media just to plan a single trip. Our mission is to simplify this entire process by bringing everything together into one intelligent platform.

With Travel Genie, users can go from **"I want to travel"** to **"My trip is fully planned"** in just a few minutes.

##  What Travel Genie Will Do

-  AI-powered destination recommendations
-  Personalized day-wise itineraries
-  Smart budget estimation
-  Real-time weather insights
-  Packing recommendations
-  Google Maps integration
-  Café & restaurant recommendations
-  Hidden gems & local attractions
-  Save and manage trips
-  Redirect to trusted booking platforms for flights, hotels, and transport

##  Vision

To become the world's most trusted AI travel companion, helping people plan smarter, travel stress-free, and explore with confidence—all from a single platform.
